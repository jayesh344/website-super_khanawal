Our Story

A dhaba table, kept honest for 15 years.

Super Khanawal started as a single roadside counter with four benches and one tawa. The recipes haven't changed since — recipes that were figured out by our founder's mother, cooked with mustard oil, hand-ground masalas, and a lot of patience. What's changed is the room around it: real tables, real cutlery, and the same food you'd get called home for.

Fresh, not frozen

Every gravy is started from scratch each morning — nothing sits overnight.

Family recipes

Passed down, not printed — three generations of the same masala box.

Quick counter service

Order to table in under 12 minutes, even during the lunch rush.
